## Interactions

### Hover Effects
- Nav links: Red underline slides in
- Gallery items: Lift up + shadow + border glow
- Buttons: Background color shift + subtle lift
- Social icons: Red background fill

### Scroll Animations
- All sections fade in from below as you scroll
- IntersectionObserver (modern, performant)
- Staggered timing for visual interest

### Mobile Behavior
- Hamburger menu slides in from right
- Gallery goes to 2 columns (mobile) → 3 (tablet) → 4 (desktop)
- Form fields stack vertically
- Tip buttons stack vertically
